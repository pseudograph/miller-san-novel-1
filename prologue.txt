This is a novel starring Mike Miller from 3ANET's "Minna no Nihongo" Japanese textbook.
The vocabulary and grammar used are what is taught in "Minna no Nihongo - Elementary 1 and 2". This novel also makes use of the sample conversations and sentences as well as practice problems from Minna no Nihongo.
Please enjoy this novel while also revising what you have learned.
